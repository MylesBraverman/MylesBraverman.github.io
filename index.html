Computer Science:

My current studies:

Right now, I am taking two computer science courses - CS321 and CS343

CS231 is all about software engineering. I am learning the actual coding side of building software, including html, css, javascript, flask, and git. And, equally as important, I am also learning about the software development process itself, including the product life cycle, version control, and different project management workflows. Over the course of the semester, I am working with a team to build a real world software solution from the ground up, giving me hands on experience and practice with all of these concepts.

CS343 is about neural networks. I am learning the theory, algorithms, and components of foundational neural network models. We dive into the details of net inputs, activation functions, weights, biases, loss gradients, etc., and how the implementations vary between different model types. We hand code each of these models, step by step, tuning hyperparameters and ultimately, training our models with real world datasets.
